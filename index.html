Mental Health

Leveraging technology towards the enhancement of community-wide mental resilience

The World Health Organization (WHO) conceptualizes mental health as a â€œstate of well-being in which the individual realizes his or her own abilities, can cope with the normal stresses of life, can work productively and fruitfully, and is able to make a contribution to his or her community
In this hackathon, we aim to explore and prototype Tech based solutions towards good mental health and mental resilience.

Areas to tackle

  • Ease and Access to services
  • Education/Awareness
  • Analytic tools

  • Therapy or Assistance
  • Caregiver Care and Support
  • Or any other areas you can identify!

Ask Question?

Let us know if you have any questions!

Is this hackathon only for Muslims?
Not at all! This event is indeed running in a mosque and we do take into account prayer times during this hackathon, but all are welcomed here!
Do I need to be a techie to join?
No specific skillsets are necessary. This event will be a great time to learn
What do I need to bring to the event?
You would need a laptop, that should be all! If you have limitations on getting a laptop, do let us know!
Do I need to stay overnight?
Staying overnight is not compulsory. If you need to leave the event, do let us know and please rejoin the event the next day at 9am for the group checkins.
Do I need to make an application?
The solution does not need to be an app or a webapp but we do encourage leveraging technologies.
Is there an age limit for this event?
This event is for age 18 and above
How do I get to the event?
You can alight at Somerset MRT and walk a short distance to the back of The Heeren.
Is there any entrance fee?
No entrace fee necessary!
